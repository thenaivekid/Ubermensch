Objective of this app

If you want the truth, this whole thing is created to remind myself of the things I believe must be remembered by every gentleman and lady. Rest is just an excuse.I have found myself depressed because of loves, self-doubts and other stuffs. So, I want to show you what I learnt from my experiences of love, my obeservation of relationships and from the uncountable books I have read. This, I hope, will help me realize my fears, goals, recent activities and knowledge that I am gaining everyday. This is created to make myself an Ubermensch as Friedrich Neitsche said. This will help me become a better man, help me have healthier relationships and help me live in the moment right here in this reality without denying my fate.

I am confident that these purposes will be served to you too by my fun little project.
